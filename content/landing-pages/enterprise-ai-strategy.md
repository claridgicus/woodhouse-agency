# Enterprise AI Strategy & Discovery

Most enterprises make one of two mistakes with AI agents: they move too fast and build the wrong thing, or they wait too long and cede ground to competitors who didn't.

The Strategy & Discovery engagement exists to eliminate both risks.

In four weeks, you will know exactly:

- Which of your workflows are worth automating — and which aren't
- What a realistic agent deployment would cost and deliver
- What the ROI looks like in month 3, month 12, and year 3
- What the right sequencing is for your organisation

That's enough to make a confident build decision — or a confident decision not to build.

---

## What's included

### Week 1 — Workflow Discovery

We work with your team to map the candidate workflows in scope. This is structured, not open-ended: we focus on volume, repeatability, and automatable fraction.

Deliverables:
- Workflow inventory (process name, volume, current cost, team size)
- Initial suitability scores

### Week 2 — Deep-Dive Assessment

For the top 3–5 workflows, we go deeper: shadowing your team, reviewing documentation, mapping data sources and system touchpoints.

Deliverables:
- Detailed workflow maps with automatable vs. human-only breakdown
- Integration complexity assessment
- Risk and compliance flags

### Week 3 — ROI Modelling

We build a financial model for each shortlisted workflow: conservative, base, and optimistic scenarios. The model is transparent — you can interrogate every assumption.

Deliverables:
- ROI model (Excel / Google Sheets)
- Payback period calculation
- 3-year NPV comparison (agent layer vs. headcount equivalent)

### Week 4 — Roadmap & Presentation

We synthesise the findings into a board-ready presentation and a prioritised deployment roadmap.

Deliverables:
- Executive summary (2 pages)
- Full assessment report
- Prioritised roadmap with sequencing rationale
- Board presentation deck (10–15 slides)
